Balatro

🎲 Balatro: Presentation & Guide

Introduction to Balatro — an innovative solo card game that combines strategy, probabilities and a unique scoring mechanic. Inspired by poker games and deck-building mechanics, Balatro immerses you in an experience where every hand is an opportunity to maximise your gains and beat your records. It's a Roguelike.

📖 Key Features

  • Poker Mechanics: Build poker hands to score points.
  • Joker System: Buy upgrades and jokers to boost your scores.
  • Campaign Mode: Progress through unique tiers and challenges.
  • Retro Visuals: A clean and colourful visual style.
